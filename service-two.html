Our Services

Residential Solar

Smart solar systems tailored for homeowners to reduce electricity bills and increase property value with clean energy.

Commercial & Industrial

High-performance solar installations built for businesses and industries seeking energy independence and operational savings.

Solar Panel Installation

Professional installation services ensuring optimal system performance, safety, & long-term reliability across all roof types.

Scalable Solutions

Flexible solar systems designed to grow with your energy needs and adapt to future expansion, from single units to full-site rollouts.

Battery Storage Systems

Store surplus solar energy with advanced battery solutions, providing reliable backup power and maximising self-consumption around the clock.

Energy Monitoring

Real-time dashboards & smart meters that track production, consumption, and savings giving you full visibility solar system's performance.

Maintenance & Servicing

Scheduled inspections, panel cleaning, and fault diagnostics to keep your system operating at peak efficiency for decades.

Connection & Net Metering

Seamless grid tie-in with net metering setup, allowing you to sell excess electricity back to the grid and reduce your utility bills further.

Solar Financing & Incentives

Navigate government rebates, tax credits, and flexible financing plans that make solar adoption affordable with minimal upfront investment.

EV Charging Integration

Pair your solar system with smart EV charging stations, letting you power your vehicle directly from the sun at home or at your business.

Custom Solar Roof Design

Architectural-grade solar integration designed to complement your building's aesthetics from flush rooftop panels to full solar tile systems.

Off-Grid Solar Systems

Complete energy independence with off-grid solar setups ideal for remote properties, farms, and locations without reliable utility access.
